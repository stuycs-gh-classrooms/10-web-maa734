The Scroll

The scroll is at the top of the violin and is curved connecting all the pieces of the violin. It keeps the strings tight and can have patterns on it sometimes. It is shaped a particular way for the sound to be the way it is. The scroll is the top most piece of the violin and is also used to hold the pegs.
